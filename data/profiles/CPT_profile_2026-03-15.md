## 🏢 CPT คืออะไร?

Camden Property Trust, an S&P 500 Company, is a real estate company. The firm is primarily engaged in the ownership, management, development, redevelopment, acquisition, and construction of multifamily apartment communities. Camden owns and operates 172 properties containing 58,759 apartment homes across the United States. Upon completion of 3 properties currently under development, the Company's portfolio will increase to 59,921 apartment homes in 175 properties.
